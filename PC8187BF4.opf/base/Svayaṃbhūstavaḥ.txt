svayaṃbhūstavaḥnamaste viśvarūpāya jyotīrūpāya te namaḥ |namaḥ svayaṃbhave nityaṃ jagaduddhārahetave

tvaṃ buddhastvaṃ ca dharmo daśabalatanayastvaṃ tathā bodhisattva-stvaṃ bhikṣuḥ śrāvakastvaṃ
kuliśavaradharastvaṃ tathā dharmadhātuḥ |tvaṃ brahmā tvaṃ ca viṣṇuḥ pramathagaṇapatistvaṃ
mahendro yamastvaṃtvaṃ pāśī tvaṃ dhaneśastvamanalapavanau nairṛtastvaṃ maheśaḥ

bhūtāḥ pretāścatiryak tvamamaramaditimārnavāstvaṃ vayaṃ cacāturyonistvameva triguṇavaratanuḥ
pañcajñānaikamūrtiḥ |varṇāstvaṃ kālamāsā dinamapi rajanī pañcabhūtāstvamevaannaṃ ratnaṃ ca
sarvaṃ matirati mahatī naḥ sadā tvāṃ natāḥ smaḥ

pañcajñānena buddhān sṛjasi svayamatho bodhisattvāṃśca pañca-bhūtānetān guṇāṃstrīnajaharigiriśān
sthāvarāñjaṅgamāṃśca |sarveṣāṃ cetasi stho naṭayasi sakalaṃ sarvato rakṣako'sitvaṃ bījaṃ
cāṅkurastvaṃ phalamapi viṭapī sarvadā tvāṃ natāḥ smaḥ

śreṣṭhaṃ kṣatraṃ tvamasmin prabhavasi bhagavān sarvataḥ sarvadevāngrāmāṃstīrthāni deśān nṛpasahitanarān
naigamāṃścāpi sarvān |dvīpeṣvanyeṣvapi tvaṃ vibhajasi sakalaṃ jyotiṣāṃ saṃvibhāgambījībhūtaikadīpo'syakhilamapi
jagadvayāpakastvāṃ natāḥ smaḥ

jyotistvadīyaṃ parito visāri sitāruṇaśyāmakapītaraktaṃdṛṣṭaṃ tataḥ sarvamidaṃ bhavantaṃ manyāmahe
tvāṃ praṇatāḥ sma nityam

nutiṃ mahārājakṛtāṃ ye paṭhiṣyanti mānavāḥ |cakravartipadaṃ prāpya te hi muktimavāpnuyuḥ

śrīcāturmahārājakṛtaṃ svayaṃbhūstavaṃ samāptam |
